TechWise – Step-by-Step Guide

Software Development

Learn product/service creation.

Tips:

  • Consult pricing of products from developers (e.g., Mr App).
  • Understand the product features and target audience.
  • Plan your unique selling point to increase value.
🤝

Middleman Opportunities

Connect creators with buyers.

Tips:

  • Find people looking for the same product Mr App sells.
  • If Mr App charges R5000 for a website, offer it at R7500 and keep R2500.
  • Build trust with both clients and developers for repeat business.
📈

Marketing & Growth

Run campaigns to maximize profit.

Tips:

  • Use social media platforms to attract more users.
  • Leverage AI tools for customer engagement and support.
  • More users → More clients → Higher revenue.
  • Track performance and optimize campaigns regularly.
